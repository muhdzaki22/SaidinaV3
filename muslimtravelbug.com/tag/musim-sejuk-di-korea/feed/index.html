Arrival In Incheon International Airport And Meeting With Tour Guide At The Airport. Lunch. Transfer To Nami Island & Tour. After that visit Petite France. Petite France is a French style park that constructed in July 2008 and popular as runningman shooting location. Dinner and after That, Transfer To Ski Resort. Check In Hotel & Rest.

DAY 2 : SKI RESORT – STRAWBERRY FARM – CHEESE MAKING – DONGDAEMUN (B/L/D)

Breakfast At Hotel And Enjoy The Ski Experience In One Of The Largest Ski Resort In Korea. Lunch. Then Transfer To Strawberry Farm (Testing Strawberry & Plugging Experience). After That, Transfer To Sheep Farm And Enjoy Making Cheese Experience.  Visit the Dongdaemun district night market. Dinner And Check In Hotel.

DAY 3 : SEOUL CITY TOUR (B/L/D)

Breakfast At Hotel. Start Your Days By Visiting Gyeongbok Palace & National Folk Museum. We Will Be Passing Blue House President. After That Experience Korean Korean Ginseng In Ginseng Outlet And Learn More About Cosmetic In Skin Care Cosmetic Center. Shopping Session In Duty Free Shop and visit Namsan Hanok Village. End your day with the visit to Namsan Tower (N Seoul Tower). Dinner And Check In Hotel.

DAY 4 : HALF DAY NAMSAN DISTRICT TOUR – FREE & EASY (B/L/NO D)

Breakfast At Hotel. Visit Amethyst Factory And and then, enjoy the scenery in Iteawon Street and visit the only Mosque in Seoul (Seoul Mosque).

Start Your Free & Easy Activity with Shopping in Namdaemun & Myeongdong Shopping Street.

DAY 5 : DEPARTURE (B)

Breakfast At Hotel. Transfer To Korean Local Market Shop.
